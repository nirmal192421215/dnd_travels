System Queries (FAQ)

Clear information regarding security deposits, driver charges, and state permits.

What are the documents needed for self-drive vehicle rentals?

For self-drive rentals, you must provide a valid Indian Driving License (LVM), Aadhaar card, and a brief verification callback. Security deposits vary based on the class of vehicle selected.

Are drivers included in the Tempo Traveller bookings?

Yes, all our luxury customized Tempo Traveller and bus rentals are strictly chauffeur-driven. Our professional drivers have extensive experience traveling across the hilly terrain of Ooty, Kodaikanal, and Yercaud.

Does the pricing include state toll tax and interstate permits?

All Tamil Nadu state permits and tourist taxes are included in our packages. If you are crossing state borders (e.g. to Kerala, Karnataka, or Pondicherry), the custom permit fees will be updated on your WhatsApp bill quote.

What is the cancellation and refund policy?

Cancellations made 48 hours prior to the scheduled pickup time receive a 100% refund. Cancellations inside 48 hours attract a minor 20% system booking retention charge.
